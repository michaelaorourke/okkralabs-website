# Website PRD — Tech Services Site

## Purpose
A modern marketing site to generate qualified leads for a product engineering consulting company.

## Primary Goals
1. Establish credibility and trust quickly
2. Clearly explain services and positioning
3. Convert visitors into booked calls or email leads

## Target Audience
- Startup founders
- CTOs / Heads of Engineering
- Product leaders
- SMB and mid‑market companies

## Key Value Proposition
We partner with teams to design, build, and scale digital products and improve existing systems.

## Conversion Actions
Primary:
- Book a call
- Send contact form

Secondary:
- Explore services
- Review case studies

## Required Pages
- Home
- Services
- Work (case studies)
- About
- Contact

## Success Metrics
- Contact form submissions
- Booked calls
- Time on site
- Scroll depth
