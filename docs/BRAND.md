# Brand & Design Guidelines

## Design Style
Inspired by Vercel, Linear, Stripe:
- Dark theme
- Minimal
- High contrast typography
- Subtle grid background
- Rounded corners
- Clean spacing

## Tone of Voice
Professional, confident, calm.
Avoid hype or buzzwords.

## Messaging Style
Good:
- Clear
- Direct
- Trustworthy
- Senior engineering tone

Avoid:
- Marketing fluff
- Overpromising
- Buzzwords

## Visual Principles
- Lots of whitespace
- Clear hierarchy
- Simple layouts
- Minimal colors
- Typography first
